Games

Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Combining precise gunplay with unique agent abilities, Valorant offers a fast-paced and strategic gaming experience.

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a classic multiplayer first-person shooter developed by Valve Corporation. Known for its highly competitive gameplay, CS:GO pits terrorists against counter-terrorists in objective-based matches.

Rainbow Six Siege

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is a team-based tactical shooter developed by Ubisoft. Set in a world of counterterrorism operations, players assume the roles of operators from various special forces units and engage in intense close-quarters combat.

Escape From Tarkov

Escape From Tarkov is a hardcore and realistic online first-person action RPG/Simulator with MMO features and a story-driven walkthrough. Players must explore the fictional city of Tarkov, scavenge for resources, engage in gunfights, and complete objectives while navigating a dynamic and dangerous environment.

Tom Clancy's Ghost Recon Wildlands

Tom Clancy's Ghost Recon Wildlands is a tactical shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Paris. Set in a massive open world, players take on the role of Ghosts, elite special operations soldiers, and embark on missions to dismantle the Santa Blanca drug cartel in Bolivia. The game features both single-player and cooperative multiplayer gameplay.

Payday 2

Payday 2 is a cooperative first-person shooter video game developed by Overkill Software and published by 505 Games. Players assume the role of criminals performing bank heists, robberies, and other various crimes as they work together to complete objectives and evade law enforcement. The game supports up to four players in multiplayer mode.
